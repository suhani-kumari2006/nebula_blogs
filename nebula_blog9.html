A Note to My Juniors

Dear Juniors,

Welcome to this new and exciting journey of engineering. As you begin this phase, I’d like to share a few thoughts based on my experience in the first semester. College is not just about books and marks – it’s about learning from every person you meet, especially your seniors and teachers.

What I Got from My Seniors

  • Guidance: From selecting the right books to preparing for practicals and exams, seniors were always there to guide me through.
  • Support: During difficult times, they supported not just academically but emotionally too. They made sure we felt welcome and safe.
  • Resources: Notes, previous year question papers, sample programs – seniors always had a way of sharing resources that helped a lot.
  • Motivation: Listening to their experiences inspired me to do better and stay focused.

What I Got from My Teachers

  • Knowledge: Teachers helped me understand complex topics in simple ways. Their experience made learning easier and more enjoyable.
  • Discipline: They helped me become more regular and responsible towards studies and time management.
  • Encouragement: Whether it was a small doubt or a big mistake, they always encouraged me to ask questions and keep improving.
  • Life Lessons: More than academics, they taught us how to stay calm, focused, and respectful in every situation.

What I Expect From You

  • Be Curious: Never hesitate to ask questions. Whether to seniors or teachers, be open to learning.
  • Be Respectful: Respect your seniors and teachers. A good attitude opens doors to great help and guidance.
  • Be Helpful: Support each other. What you learn today, share tomorrow.
  • Be Responsible: Stay serious about your goals and actions. Your choices will shape your journey.

College will give you memories, friends, lessons, and a path to your dreams. Make the most of it. We, your seniors, are always here to help. Don't be afraid to reach out!

Wishing you all the best for your journey ahead!

With warm regards,
Your Senior, Suhani Kumari

Internship Guide for Juniors

Simple steps to start your internship journey

Hello Juniors!

If you are a 1st year student, this blog is for you. Internships help you learn real work and build your future. Let’s see how to find them.

What is an Internship Portal?

An internship portal is a website where you can find and apply for internships. You just need to create an account and apply to the companies you like.

Useful Internship Websites

  • Internshala – Best site for students, very easy to use.
  • AICTE Internship Portal – Government site for engineering students.
  • TCS iON – From Tata company, for learning and internships.
  • LetsIntern – Offers internships in companies and startups.
  • HelloIntern – Site for internships and training.
  • NCS (National Career Service) – Government career portal.

How to Use These Portals?

  1. Go to the website.
  2. Sign up using your email ID.
  3. Fill your profile – name, education, skills, resume.
  4. Search for internships (example: Marketing, Web Development, Data analyst).
  5. Click "Apply" and wait for reply.

Easy Tips to Get Internship

  • Make a simple resume.
  • Learn basic computer or coding skills.
  • Apply to more than one internship.
  • Practice speaking for interviews.
  • Take help from seniors or teachers.

Start now. Don’t wait for final year. Internships will help you learn, grow, and succeed.
